Welcome:

ClinDesk comprises a team of senior medical students and resident physicians who are passionate about opening the otherwise closed world of medical knowledge.

Soon, we'll be launching lots of Creative Commons-licensed medical resources, including:

  • Free medical education videos, through our sister-site White Coat Academy
  • Open USMLE prep materials, including a Goljan-esque Step I review series
  • An interactive physical exam tutorial featuring gunner-style facts (remember Duroziez's sign?)
  • An H&P/SOAP note wizard, with hints on important acronyms and diagnostics
  • The Vaccinate Doctors Initiative, advocating for influenza vaccines

Hopefully we can change the face of medical education. If you're interested in helping out, please get in touch!


Stay tuned,
