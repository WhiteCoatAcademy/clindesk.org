Who?

ClinDesk is a team of senior medical students and resident physicians deeply familiar with medical knowledge and education. Our experiences in medical school have shaped our view that there is a disconnect between the training of doctors and the way patients understand what is happening with their health. More

What?

ClinDesk.org is a free video library that opens medical education to patients and their supporters. Our videos are organized by medical speciality and specific disease, which walk you through the basic biology, “who and when” epidemiology, symptoms, and treatment/outcomes of each condition you or someone you know might be facing. More

Why?

Our mission is to lead the transition of medical education from a proprietary to “open” process. It’s your health, so the ability to learn about it should be yours, too! We believe medical education should be accessible to everyone, especially the people most intimately connected to medicine: patients themselves. More
