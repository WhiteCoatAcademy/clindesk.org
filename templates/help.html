The underlying principle behind everything we do at ClinDesk is “approachability” -- both the content in our videos and our site design should be straight-forward and efficient.

Everything starts at our homepage: ClinDesk.org, where you’ll find basic information about us, an introductory video to our mission, as well as a simple navigation panel that lets you identify the medical specialty you are interested in learning more about. The most commonly explored conditions within each specialty are listed beneath each specialty icon. This should help you find what specialty you’re looking for and will allow you to get to the most commonly accessed pages more quickly.

Once you’re visiting the medical specialty of interest to you, simply find the condition you’d like to learn more about and click on the link to visit the dedicated page for that condition. From there, you’ll be presented with the videos we have on that subject.

For a given condition, our videos are most often watched top-to-bottom, flowing from basic biology, to “who and when” epidemiology, to symptoms, and finally treatment/outcomes. Of course, feel free to find and watch specific videos that are most relevant to what you’re looking for.

Each video is also paired with “Quick Notes”, which are our way of getting the most essential information to you as quickly as possible. For example, the “Quick Notes” for a given condition might define a few key terms regarding the specific cells in the human body affected by this condition, the average age of onset of the condition, as well as a list of common modern treatments for the condition.

What if my doctor tells me something different?

Always listen to your doctor! Medicine is complex and extremely personal. Our understanding of diseases, diagnostics, and therapeutic options are changing daily.

ClinDesk and White Coat Academy are only guides to help you understand conditions, and barely skim the surface of medicine. A medical professional knows best!
