What if my doctor tells me something different?

Always listen to your doctor! Medicine is complex and extremely personal. Our understanding of diseases, diagnostics, and therapeutic options are changing daily.

ClinDesk and White Coat Academy are only guides to help you understand conditions, and barely skim the surface of medicine. A medical professional knows best!

Can I trust what I find on ClinDesk and White Coat Academy?

We hope so! We go to great lengths to ensure our content is accurate, but we might make some mistakes (keep in mind we're medical students!). To make sure we're as error-free as possible, we review all our videos, and our clinical advisory board checks our content.

If you've found an error or mistake, please let us know at info@clindesk.org.

Can I save or download your videos and other content? What's your copyright policy?

Yes, we encourage it -- with one caveat. We license all our content under a Creative Commons license. We've chosen the Creative Commons 3.0 BY-NC-SA License.

This means three things:

  • BY — By Attribution: You must clearly attribute any content to ClinDesk & White Coat Academy.
  • NC — Non-Commercial: You cannot use our content for commercial purposes.
  • SA — Share Alike: If you build upon our content, you need to share it with others using a similar license.
How can I give feedback? I think I found a mistake on the site.

We'd love to hear from you! if you find a topic confusing (or think we got something wrong!), e-mail us at info@clindesk.org. Keep in mind that we cannot provide any medical advice, but we'd love feedback on our videos!

How is ClinDesk financed? Do you make money?

We're a non-profit organization, which means we depend on donations and grants to keep us going (bandwidth is expensive!).

If you've found our materials helpful, please donate so we can continue to improve our resources.

I have a medical question — Can I ask you?

Unfortunately, no. You should contact a real doctor, and if you have a medical emergency, you should of course call 911 (or your country's emergency number).
